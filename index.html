About

I am a Doctoral Research Student in the Department of Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, working under the supervision of Prof. Debjani Chakraborty & Dr. Debashree Guha Adhya. My research focuses on constructing aggregation operators, with applications to medical image analysis, specifically analyzing patterns in Fundus and OCT images.

Prior to joining as a researcher, I completed M.Tech in Computer Science and Data Processing (CSDP) from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.

Research Highlights

  • Novel unsupervised method for retinal vascular segmentation using Bonferroni mean pre-aggregation and dynamic fuzzy histogram equalization, enabling accurate extraction of small vessels in low-contrast regions.
